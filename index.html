Artisan Wood and Stone work from the Isle of Man

Sticks & Stones is a small family run business based in Peel, on the Isle of Man. Our ethos is to create hand crafted items for your home that echo the beauty and character that can be found all over this island we are proud to call Home, or in Manx Gaelic Thie My Chree - "Home of my heart".

Products

At Sticks & Stones we strive to produce all of our products using raw materials that have been sourced locally, on island. In most cases, unless specifically requested, we will not use new timber nor quarried stone.

It is important to us that we recycle, reclaim and waste nothing. Our materials come from a variety of sources; beaches, industrial & residential reclamation and pre-loved items.

Our product pages are under development and we will be launching our Etsy shop in the New Year. Feel free to call or email us if you'd like to find out more.
