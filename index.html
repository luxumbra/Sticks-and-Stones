Artisan Wood and Stone work from the Isle of Man

Sticks & Stones is a small family run business based in Peel, on the Isle of Man. Our ethos is to create hand crafted items for your home that echo the beauty and character that can be found all over this island we are proud to call, Home or Thie My Chree.

Products

At Sticks & Stones we strive to produce all of our products using raw materials that have been sourced locally, on island. In most cases, unless specifically requested, we will not use new timber nor quarried stone.

It is important to us that we recycle, reclaim and waste nothing. Our materials come from a variety of sources; beaches, industrial & residential reclamation and pre-loved items.

Take a look at our products pages or head on over to Etsy to view a great selection of quality, handmade items.
